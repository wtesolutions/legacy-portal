Death Verification Process

LegacySafe requires proper verification before granting full access to your executor and implementing your legacy wishes.

Verification Requirements

  • Death certificate upload by your executor
  • Identity verification of the executor
  • Waiting period of 7 days after verification submission
  • Secondary confirmation by at least one trusted contact

What Happens After Verification

  • Death notification list contacts are notified
  • Executor receives full access to your vault
  • Your legacy instructions are provided to the executor
  • Beneficiaries receive access to designated documents
